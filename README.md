# AP Guide - Marriott

After a year in General Accounting (Accounts Payable) at Baltimore Marriott Waterfront, I'd picked up enough about the systems — HotShop, PSAP, ReadSoft, and the rest — that it was time to pass that knowledge on. This site is my way of documenting everything I learned so the next person stepping into the role doesn't have to figure it all out from scratch.

It's a collection of screen recordings and written walkthroughs covering the day-to-day AP processes: creating POs, receiving in HotShop, entering invoices in PSAP, handling exceptions, running reports, and more. Think of it as the training guide I wish I had on day one.
