# Creating Purchase Orders

How to create a new purchase order in HotShop from start to finish.

## Step 1: Open HotShop

Navigate to the HotShop portal and log in with your credentials.

{/* Standard image — just markdown, points to your R2 bucket */}
![HotShop login screen](https://r2.hla.dev/marriott/hotshop/login-screen.png)

## Step 2: Click "New Purchase Order"

From the dashboard, click the **New Purchase Order** button in the top right.

{/* Image with a caption underneath */}
![New PO button location](https://r2.hla.dev/marriott/hotshop/new-po-button.png)
*The button is in the top-right corner of the dashboard.*

## Step 3: Fill in the details

1. Select the **vendor** from the dropdown
2. Enter the **GL code** (ask your manager if unsure)
3. Add line items with descriptions and amounts
4. Double-check the **total** matches your invoice

> **Tip:** Always double-check the GL codes before submitting. Wrong GL codes are the #1 reason POs get kicked back.

## Step 4: Submit for approval

Click **Submit**. Your manager will get an email notification to approve it.

| Status | What it means |
|--------|--------------|
| **Pending** | Waiting for manager approval |
| **Approved** | Ready to send to vendor |
| **Rejected** | Check the comments for why |

## Common mistakes

- **Wrong GL code** — double check with the chart of accounts
- **Missing vendor** — if the vendor isn't in the dropdown, see [Adding a New Vendor](/hotshop/adding-vendors)
- **Duplicate PO** — search existing POs first before creating a new one
