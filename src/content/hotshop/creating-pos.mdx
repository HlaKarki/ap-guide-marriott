# Creating Purchase Orders

This is a test article to verify MDX works on Cloudflare Workers.

## Step 1: Open HotShop

Navigate to the HotShop portal and log in with your credentials.

## Step 2: Create the PO

1. Click **New Purchase Order**
2. Fill in the vendor details
3. Add line items
4. Submit for approval

> **Tip:** Always double-check the GL codes before submitting.
